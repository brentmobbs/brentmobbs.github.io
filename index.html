Bio

Brent Mobbs is an associate in the Intellectual Property Litigation Practice Group at Kirkland & Ellis LLP in New York. He concentrates on intellectual property matters, including patent disputes, trade secret misappropriation, and trademark litigation. With a strong commitment to client advocacy, he addresses complex legal challenges at the intersection of technology and law.

Brent approaches each matter by dissecting technical details, aligning legal strategies with business objectives, and delivering precise counsel. His background includes guiding clients through high-stakes IP conflicts, as well as working on transactional aspects that hinge on intellectual property rights.
