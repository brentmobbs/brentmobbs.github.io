Bio

Brent Mobbs is an associate in the Intellectual Property Litigation Practice Group at Kirkland & Ellis LLP in Los Angeles. They concentrate on intellectual property matters, including patent disputes, trade secret misappropriation, and copyright and trademark litigation. With a strong commitment to client advocacy, they address complex legal challenges at the intersection of technology and law.

Brent approaches each matter by dissecting technical details, aligning legal strategies with business objectives, and delivering precise counsel. Their background includes guiding clients through high-stakes IP conflicts, as well as advising on intellectual property rights in the absence of potential litigation.
